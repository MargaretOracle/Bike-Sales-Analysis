# Bike-Sales-Analysis

## Project Overview
This project is a deep dive into a high-end bike shop that was doing great until it hit a major speed jump. In 2016 and 2017, the business was booming,but by 2018, sales suddenly dropped by nearly ** half (47%) **

I used ** Bike Shop Dataset ** to play a detective. I wanted to find out if the shop was losing customers, running out of popular bikes, or if the staff just couldn't keep up with the work.
By lookig at three years of sales, inventor and shipping dta, i identified exactly where the money was leaking and how to plug the holes for 2019.
